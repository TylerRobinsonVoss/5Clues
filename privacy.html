Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

Overview

5 Clues ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

5 Clues is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal details
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data

Local Data Storage

Your game progress, settings, and achievements are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements through third-party ad networks. These services may collect certain information as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not control or have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

5 Clues does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
